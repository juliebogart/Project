Welcome to Happy Hooves Ranch! We have a 10 horse stables located near Tamarindo beach. We offer a variety of riding options.

Trail riding: Ride through the stunning rainforest on horseback to view all sorts of native Costa Rican animals, insects, and birds. Great for beginner riders.

Beach riding: Gallop along Tamarindo beach through the waves and ride the horses as they swim in the ocean! Great for riders of all experience levels. We will tailor the ride to your level.

Show jumping: Jump our athletic horses on our property over well maintained show jumps. Great for experienced riders who want to continue to advance.

Cross country jumping: The best of all worlds for our experienced riders. Gallop through open fields over solid jumps, down hills, and in and out of water.
